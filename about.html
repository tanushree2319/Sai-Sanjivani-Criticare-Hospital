Our Story

Caring for Kalyan, Caring for Every Life

For years, Sai Sanjeevani Criticare Hospital has been a trusted healthcare destination for families across Kalyan and surrounding regions.

Built on a foundation of clinical excellence, compassion, and ethical medical practice, the hospital provides comprehensive healthcare services under one roof. With modern medical infrastructure, experienced specialists, advanced diagnostics, critical care facilities, and 24×7 emergency support, we are committed to delivering timely, safe, and patient-focused treatment.
