Cashless TPA Partners

We have tie-ups with leading Third Party Administrators (TPA) who facilitate seamless cashless treatment for our patients. Our partnerships ensure quick authorization and hassle-free claim processing.

  • FHPL (Family Health Plan Insurance TPA Ltd.)
